About section
Caitlin Stall-Paquet is a Montreal-based writer, editor and translator whose work has appeared in Elle Canada, Air Canada enRoute, Hazlitt, Maisonneuve Magazine, Paste, Vice, Matrix, Carte Blanche, Potluck Mag, Ambos, Cult MTL and She Does the City. She was an associate editor at Air Canada enRoute, was nominated for the 3Macs carte blanche Prize and was longlisted for the CBC Short Story Prize.
